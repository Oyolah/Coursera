Shrimps

Melt 2 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant. Fry shrimp and add salt and pepper, to your taste. Add in the remaining butter, lemon juice and water. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley and serve over rice or pasta.
